What Fima uses

  • Your account and license status.
  • HWID for license binding.
  • Discord and Roblox link status if you connect them.
  • Stripe handles card payments.

What Fima does not do

  • Does not steal cookies.
  • Does not read browser passwords.
  • Does not collect your Discord token.
  • Does not collect your Roblox cookie or password.

Fake files

Modified builds are not supported. They can steal accounts, break licenses, or hide malware. If a file is not from our site or release page, do not run it.

Responsible disclosure

Found a real security problem? Open a ticket and give us the safe details. Do not share internals, bypasses, cracks, patches, decompiled code, or working exploit steps publicly.

Reverse engineering policy: high-level questions are okay, but reverse engineering, decompiling, patching, bypassing, cracking, or sharing internals is not allowed. Modified builds are not supported.
