Privacy Policy

Effective date: June 1, 2026 Website: fimamacro.com Contact: Discord support

This Privacy Policy explains how Fima Macro collects, uses and protects information when you use our website, account system, checkout, license services, downloads and support channels.

Information We Collect

  • Account information such as email address, password hash and optional Roblox username.
  • Purchase information such as selected plan, Stripe customer ID, checkout session ID, payment status and license key.
  • License information such as HWID, activation status, plan, expiration date, validation attempts and download activity.
  • Support information you voluntarily provide in Discord tickets, feedback forms or support messages.
  • Basic technical information needed for security, abuse prevention and service operation.

How We Use Information

  • To create and manage your Fima Macro account.
  • To process payments through Stripe and deliver licenses after checkout.
  • To validate license keys, bind licenses to a device HWID and prevent unauthorized sharing.
  • To provide downloads, updates, support, fraud prevention and service security.
  • To improve the website, app experience and support process.

Payments

Card payments are processed by Stripe. Fima Macro does not store full card numbers or bank details on our servers. Stripe may process payment data according to its own privacy practices.

Roblox And Discord

If you provide a Roblox username or use Discord support, we may use that information to help identify your account, ticket or manual payment request. Fima Macro is not affiliated with, endorsed by or sponsored by Roblox Corporation.

Cookies And Local Storage

The website may store preferences such as theme, language and currency in your browser. Account sessions may use secure cookies or tokens to keep you logged in.

Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We share data only with service providers needed to operate the service, such as Stripe for payments, hosting providers for infrastructure and Discord when you choose to use support.

Security

We use reasonable technical measures such as password hashing, server-side license validation, HTTPS and restricted admin access. No online service can guarantee absolute security.

Retention

We keep account, order and license records for as long as needed to provide access, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, prevent abuse and operate the product.

Your Choices

You may contact support to request help with your account, license, HWID reset or data-related questions. Some records may need to be retained for payment, security or legal reasons.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy when our website, app, payment flow or license system changes. The latest version will be available at this page.
