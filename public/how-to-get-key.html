License guide

How to get your FIMA key

Use this page when the app asks where to get a key. It shows the safe path: choose a plan, finish checkout, copy your masked license from Fima, then paste it into the app once. Never buy from fake links or share your full key.

Quick checklist

1

Buy, earn, or redeem. Paid keys come from checkout. Activity Reward and gift keys show in your account after they are granted or redeemed.

2

Copy from Fima only. Use the success page or My Products. Keys are masked on-screen for privacy.

3

Paste into the app. The app binds access to your account/device and stores a safe entitlement instead of showing the full key again.

4

Need help? If payment worked but the key is missing, open support with your masked order/key details only.

Step-by-step

These steps work for 3-day access, monthly access, lifetime access, gift/redeem keys and Activity Rewards. The flow may look slightly different depending on whether you are buying, receiving a leaderboard reward or redeeming a gift.

01

Go to Pricing

Open fimamacro.com/pricing, choose the plan you want, and start checkout. Do not use old SellAuth or unofficial links.

02

Finish checkout or redeem

Stripe opens for paid plans. Gift, redeem and Activity Reward flows stay inside your Fima account so the key is linked safely.

03

Copy your key

After checkout, use the success page or My Products. The page shows a masked key and a Copy button.

04

Activate in the app

Open FIMA MACRO, paste the key, and press Activate License. If your device changed, support can help with HWID reset.

Security note: Fima never needs your Roblox password, cookies, Discord token, or browser session token. Only enter your license key inside the official Fima app or website.

If something goes wrong

Most activation problems are account, payment, or device-binding issues. Use masked details when asking for help.

I paid but cannot find my key

Open My Products. If it is still missing, contact support with the payment time and masked account info.

The app says the key is for another device

Your HWID may have changed. Open support and request a reset. Do not post full HWID or full key in public channels.

The app asks me to validate again

Make sure you are using the latest approved build. If it repeats, open a ticket and mention "license refresh loop".
